Transformation Through Collaboration

My career began in Rio de Janeiro managing import operations, where I learned that procurement success depends on relationships built on trust, not just transactions. This philosophy carried me through LATAM expansion for Ford Lighting, where I coordinated distributors across 12 countries, and eventually to leading premium product development for world-renowned brands like Pininfarina, Blaupunkt, and Black+Decker across Europe and Asia.

"I don't just optimize supply chains—I build cross-functional coalitions that transform how organizations think about procurement. My approach: trust-first, data-informed, results-focused."

Today, I combine deep procurement expertise with cutting-edge AI and data science to deliver transformational results. At Details Hospitality, I didn't just achieve €1M+ in cost savings within 6 months—I rebuilt the entire procurement framework from the ground up, creating systems that empower teams to make better decisions faster. I developed proprietary BidMap templates, enhanced RFP/RFQ processes, aligned technical specifications with operational needs, and implemented exhaustive project follow-up protocols that ensure complete transparency across all stakeholders.

My work extends beyond procurement—I'm currently leading a transversal initiative with Marketing, Technical, and Operations teams that's being hailed as a major success, demonstrating that procurement can be a strategic partner rather than just a cost center.
